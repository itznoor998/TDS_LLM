Pomodoro Technique Tutorial

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that can help improve your focus and productivity. Follow these steps:

  1. Set a timer for a specific period (traditionally 25 minutes).
  2. Work on a task until the timer rings.
  3. Take a short break (5 minutes) after the timer goes off.
  4. Repeat the process. After completing four sessions, take a longer break (15-30 minutes).

Use this timer to structure your work and take meaningful breaks to maintain productivity!
